01
Category 1

Professional Security Suite

NK

Anti-Nuke

Monitors mass bans, kicks, channel deletion, and role deletion, then strips dangerous roles from suspected nukers.

RD

Anti-Raid

Detects join spikes, logs raid alerts, locks down everyone send permissions, and kicks very new accounts during raids.

SP

Anti-Spam and Anti-Link

Rate-based spam detection, repeated-message checks, auto-deletes, warnings, cooldown cleanup, and channel-level anti-link bypasses.

AI

AI Toxicity Detection

Uses Gemini-powered moderation signals to detect harmful content patterns that simple keyword rules often miss.

02
Category 2

Gemini AI Intelligence

CH

AI Chatbot

A dedicated AI channel with configurable server context so the bot responds with the right tone for your community.

WR

AI Weekly Recap

Every Sunday, the bot generates a recap of key discussions for configured channels and tracks the latest recap run.

TG

AI Auto-Tagging

Analyzes new forum threads and applies matching tags automatically in configured forum channels.

TR

AI Translation

Translates a specific message by ID inside the current channel for easier multilingual support.

SM

AI Summaries and Status

Summarizes the last 100 messages and exposes AI stamina, cooldown, active model, and request stats with fallback handling.

AI quota note

AI-powered features depend on Gemini availability and free-tier quota. When quota is exhausted, AI chat, summaries, translations, recaps, and toxicity analysis may slow down, enter cooldown, or temporarily stop until quota resets.

Premium Member Experience

Welcome and Invite Tracking

Category 4

Advanced Moderation and Utility

MD

Full Mod Suite

Ban, unban by ID, kick, mute, unmute, warn, warnings, clear, purge user, lock, unlock, set nick, and slowmode.

LG

Professional Logging

A dedicated log channel records moderation actions, audit events, joins, leaves, edits, deletes, anti-raid alerts, and anti-nuke actions.

EM

Embeds and Reminders

Send custom embeds, edit bot embeds by message ID, and run reminder alerts through a scheduled checker.

IF

Info Commands

Use info, serverinfo, userinfo, roleinfo, avatar, ping, help, and 8ball to support both staff and members.

05
Category 5

Transparency and Health

HP

Categorized Help

A clean help system keeps a large command surface easy to understand even though the bot currently lives in a single index.js file.

ST

AI Status and Stored Config

/aistatus exposes runtime health while data.json stores per-server settings like logs, warnings, AI context, recaps, birthdays, and reminders.

Free quota behavior

Some AI features are limited by free quota. If the quota is reached, the bot can fall back, cool down requests, or temporarily disable AI responses until service becomes available again.
